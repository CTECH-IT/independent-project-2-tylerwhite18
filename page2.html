Food Banks

When you donate to AWMH, most of the funds are distributed to food banks and food packaging events worldwide. Locally and abroad, AWMH has helped hundreds of food banks ditribute millions of meals.

Metallica Scholars Program

Supporting the All Within My Hands Foundation also contributes funds to the Metallica Scholars Program, which gives funds to community colleges in order to support education for those who might not have otherwise been able to afford it. Currently, AWMH partners with 23 community colleges across the US.
